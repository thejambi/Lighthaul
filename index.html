LIGHTHAUL

A RELATIVISTIC COURIER GAME

Haul cargo and passengers between stations at a hair under light speed — and let special relativity be the economy.

Fuel is Δv — the tank is small and you pay again to brake, so fly only as fast as the job needs.
Deadlines run on universe time — cruise too slowly and you miss them.
You and your passengers age on ship time — fly high-γ to stay young. Retire rich, not old.
