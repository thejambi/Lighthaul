LIGHTHAUL

A RELATIVISTIC COURIER GAME

Haul cargo and passengers between stations at a hair under light speed. Fuel is Δv — it diverges toward c, the tank is small, and you pay again to brake, so fly only as fast as the job needs. Deadlines run on universe time · passengers age on ship time (keep γ high) · and so do you — retire rich, not old. Your inertial dampers cancel the brutal G of cruising, but hard burns and hard braking still stress the load — respect each contract's inertial rating or arrive with damaged goods.
